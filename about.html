ABOUT

Tiger talks was started by Maria Fernando to spread her love for art with everyone. Maria is a self taught artist from Napoli, Italy. She has an art galery, Artista, in Naples, New York, France, Rome, Florence and Milan. Connect with her on twitter, instagram or drop by to her gallery.

This blog is a forum for budding as well as professional artists looking for a friendly community to discuss various topics like sketching, art supplies and paintings. We also support artists by giving them a shout out. Send us a message or email us with you art for a shoutout.
